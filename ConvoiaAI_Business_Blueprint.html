The Problem We Solve

Businesses today face a fragmented AI landscape. They need GPT for writing, Claude for analysis, Gemini for search, DALL-E for images, and separate subscriptions for each. Managing this across a team of 10, 50, or 500 people is a nightmare — no visibility, no cost control, no governance.

2. What is ConvoiaAI?

ConvoiaAI is a unified AI gateway platform — think of it as the "Stripe for AI models." Just as Stripe lets businesses accept payments from any card without building separate integrations, ConvoiaAI lets businesses use any AI model without managing separate API keys, billing, and access controls.

Core Value Propositions

🔀 Multi-Model Access

Access GPT-5, Claude Opus, Gemini Pro, DeepSeek, Mistral, LLaMA and 30+ more models through one chat interface. Switch models mid-conversation.

🎨 Beyond Text

Generate images (DALL-E, GPT Image, Gemini Image), create videos (Google Veo), run web searches, process PDFs — all from one place.

👥 Team Management

Invite team members, assign roles (employee, manager, owner), allocate token budgets, and track who uses what — and how much it costs.

📱 Multi-Platform

Full-featured web app, native Android app (Play Store), iOS app (App Store) — all sharing the same backend and real-time sync.

3. Market Opportunity — Why People Want This

The Pain Points

🔥 Problem 1: Subscription Sprawl

A typical knowledge worker today has: ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) + Claude Pro ($20/mo) + Gemini Advanced ($20/mo) + Midjourney ($10/mo) = $70/month per person. For a 20-person team, that's $16,800/year — with no visibility into actual usage.

🔥 Problem 2: No Cost Control

CTO buys ChatGPT Team for everyone. Marketing uses it daily, engineering barely touches it. The company pays the same $25/seat regardless. 60% of AI spend is wasted on inactive seats.

🔥 Problem 3: Model Lock-In

Teams pick one AI provider and get locked in. When a better or cheaper model launches, switching means rebuilding workflows, retraining staff, and losing conversation history. No flexibility to use the best model for each task.

🔥 Problem 4: Zero Governance

Managers have no idea what their teams are using AI for. No usage reports, no cost breakdowns, no ability to set limits. This is a compliance and budget risk especially in regulated industries.

7. Token Economics Deep Dive

Tokens are the universal currency of ConvoiaAI. Every AI interaction — chat messages, image generation, web searches, document analysis — consumes tokens. Roughly 1 token ≈ 4 characters of text (a word is ~1.3 tokens on average).

Token Wallet Architecture

  • Personal Wallet: Every user has a token balance. Individual users buy directly.
  • Organization Pool: Org owner buys tokens centrally. Allocates to members.
  • Budget Caps: Monthly limits per user. Alert at threshold. Auto-downgrade to cheaper model.
  • Transaction Logging: Every deduction logged with: user, model, tokens, cost, timestamp.
  • Audit Trail: Full history of purchases, allocations, and usage — visible to owner/admin.

11. Competitive Advantages

🏆 Moat #1: Multi-Provider Aggregation

No single AI provider can match having GPT-5, Claude Opus, Gemini Pro, DeepSeek, LLaMA, and Mistral under one roof. Users don't need to manage 6 separate accounts, billing systems, and API keys. This is the "Stripe effect" — abstract complexity, provide one interface.

🏆 Moat #2: Enterprise Token Economy

The combination of token budgets + allocation + auto-downgrade + audit trails is unique. No competitor offers per-user budget caps with automatic model fallback. This is a CFO's dream — predictable AI costs with zero waste.

🏆 Moat #3: AI Agent Ecosystem

13 domain-expert agents + unlimited custom agents create a "virtual workforce" that makes the platform sticky. Once a company builds custom agents with institutional knowledge, switching costs become very high.

🏆 Moat #4: Usage-Based Pricing

While competitors charge $20-25/seat/month regardless of usage, ConvoiaAI starts at $1 and scales linearly. A 50-person company where only 10 people use AI daily pays for actual usage — not 50 seats. This is 60-70% cheaper for most organizations.

Why Now?

  • AI model proliferation: GPT-5, Claude 4, Gemini 3 all launched in 2025-2026. Companies can't evaluate all of them individually.
  • Enterprise AI adoption: 78% of Fortune 500 companies have AI policies now. They need governance tools.
  • Cost pressure: AI spend is growing 40%+ YoY. CFOs demand visibility and control.
  • Open-source quality leap: LLaMA 3.3, Mixtral, DeepSeek are now competitive with proprietary models. Customers want access to both.
